Chicken livers pate

Price: R120

Serving size: 1 tbsp (13g)

Cicken livers pate are livers that are trimmed of their connective tissues,sauted in butter with shallots,garlic,capers and a litle brandy and then puree with cream and a litle more butter. It's best serve chilled, and because of its richness, a little goes a long way.
